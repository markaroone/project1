Frequently Asked Questions

01

We love your work, what is the next step?

Awesome! I take bookings on a first-come, first serve basis. So if you like what you see, drop me a message via the contact page to check my availability and get the convo started.

02

What are your travel costs?

I work all over the Philippines and internationally so am used to travelling all over. Don't worry, like my beer, I keep it cheap. For weddings 100 km or more from Manila, PH you may need to cover my travel and accommodation. This will be agreed upon booking and all travel arrangements are booked by myself.

03

Can we meet before the big day?

Sure. I am always game to meet new friends over a beer or two. If you are ever in Manila, drop me a line. For those further afield, we can sink our beers over a Zoom sesh.

04

Do you take family pics too?

If you want them, I got you covered. Yeah, they may not be the coolest or most creative shots ever but for me, they are the legacy photos your future family will cherish. Ahead of your wedding, I will send across a call sheet of the day to finalise all the details and special requests.
